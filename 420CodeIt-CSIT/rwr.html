CMPS 285

Software Engineering

In this course, Students will assemble a team of 3 to 6 students and work on a project of their choosing.
Students will initially choose a client for whom they will develop a program of their choosing.
Afterward, students will give a demonstration to show what the final product is expected to do.
Presentations will be given throughout the cycle of the chosen project's development.

CMPS 383

Information Systems

Students will become familiar with the file structure, organization, and inner workings of information systems
such as web applications through the development of a term project. Students go through five phases of development
as they progress through the course. Starting out, students will be familiar with online project collaboration applications
such as GitHub and BitBucket. They will learn through the first four phases how to design and develop
information systems for a select company. The fifth and final phase, students will develop a full blown application
designed to meet the needs of the company in question and may deploy it at the end of the semester
